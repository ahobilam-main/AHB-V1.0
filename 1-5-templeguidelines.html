Strict Temple Guidelines

Electronics Ban

  • NO CELL PHONES ALLOWED. Devices are completely banned beyond the main entrance.
  • Photography and videography are strictly prohibited inside the premises.
  • Violators will face immediate device confiscation and disciplinary action.

Mandatory Dress Code

Men

  • Dhoti, Pancha, Kurta, or Formal Wear.
  • Shorts, sleeveless shirts, and torn clothing banned.

Women

  • Saree, Half-Saree, or Chudidar with Dupatta.
  • Western wear, short tops, and tight clothing banned.

General Conduct

  • All footwear must be completely removed outside the temple boundaries.
  • Maintain absolute divine silence. Chanting must be done internally.
  • Keep the premises clean. Plastic covers and bags are strictly banned.
  • Maintain queue discipline peacefully. Pushing is not tolerated.
