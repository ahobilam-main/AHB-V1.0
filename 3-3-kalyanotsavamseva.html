Required Pooja Items

  • Loose Flowers - 20 Cubits (20 Mooralu Pulu)
  • 2 Coconuts
  • Turmeric & Kumkum (Pasupu & Kumkuma)
  • Betel Leaves & Nuts (Akulu & Vakkalu)

Important Note

Kalyanotsavam is not performed if any of the following falls on the scheduled day:

  • Amavasya (New Moon)
  • Pournami (Full Moon)
  • Ekadashi
  • Swathi Nakshatra

On such occasions, the Kalyanotsavam is strictly not conducted.
