3 Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Kalyanotsavam Every Sunday Upper & Lower Ahobilam Conducted subject to the temple festival calendar.

Important Note

Kalyanotsavam is not performed if any of the following falls on the scheduled day:

  • Amavasya (New Moon)
  • Pournami (Full Moon)
  • Ekadashi
  • Swathi Nakshatra

On such occasions, the Kalyanotsavam is strictly not conducted.
