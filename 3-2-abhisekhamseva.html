Abhishekam Sevas

1. Monthly Abhishekam

Occasion / Date: Every Amavasya

Location: Upper & Lower Ahobilam

2. Monthly Abhishekam

Occasion / Date: Every Pournami (Full Moon)

Location: Upper & Lower Ahobilam

3. Monthly Abhishekam

Occasion / Date: Every Ekadashi (Twice every month)

Location: Upper & Lower Ahobilam

4. Swathi Nakshatra Abhishekam

Occasion / Date: Every Swathi Nakshatra

Location: Upper & Lower Ahobilam

5. Moolavar Abhishekam

Occasion / Date: Every Swathi Nakshatra

Location: Upper & Lower Ahobilam

6. Nava Narasimha Abhishekam

Occasion / Date: Every Swathi Nakshatra

Location: All Nine Narasimha Temples

7. Dasara Navaratri Abhishekam

Occasion / Date: During 10 Days of Dasara Navaratri

Location: Upper & Lower Ahobilam

8. Narasimha Jayanthi Abhishekam

Occasion / Date: During Narasimha Jayanthi Festival (May)

Location: Upper & Lower Ahobilam

9. Jyeshtabhishekam

Occasion / Date: Annual Festival (Jyeshta Month)

Location: Upper & Lower Ahobilam
