Chief Administrative Officer / GPA Ahobilam

Welcome to Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Devasthanam, Ahobilam, the sacred abode of Lord Lakshmi Narasimha and one of India's most revered spiritual destinations. We are honored to welcome devotees from across the world who visit this holy kshetra seeking divine blessings, inner peace, and spiritual fulfillment. Our administration is dedicated to preserving the temple's sacred traditions and rich heritage while continuously improving pilgrim amenities, maintaining cleanliness, ensuring safety, and providing a smooth and peaceful darshan experience. We humbly request every devotee to respect temple customs, uphold the sanctity of the shrine, and cooperate in maintaining the cleanliness and serenity of this holy place. Your support helps preserve the timeless legacy of Ahobilam for future generations. May the boundless grace of Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy bless you and your family with good health, happiness, strength, and everlasting peace.

|| OM NAMO LAKSHMI NRUSIMHAYA ||

Ahobilam Temple, nestled amidst the majestic Nallamala Hills of Andhra Pradesh, is one of the most sacred pilgrimage destinations dedicated to Lord Lakshmi Narasimha, the fourth incarnation of Lord Vishnu. According to ancient scriptures, this is the divine place where Lord Narasimha manifested to protect His devoted भक्त Prahlada and destroy the demon king Hiranyakashipu, symbolizing the triumph of righteousness over evil. Revered as one of the 108 Divya Desams, Ahobilam is home to the sacred Nava Narasimha temples, each representing a unique form of the Lord. Surrounded by lush forests, hills, and natural caves, the temple offers a deeply spiritual experience. Its timeless traditions, rich heritage, and serene atmosphere continue to inspire millions of devotees seeking divine blessings, peace, courage, and spiritual fulfillment.
